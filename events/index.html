III · The Standard

Who shows up.

The bench belongs to a private mixology studio, and it is held to the studio's standard - not a staffing agency's roster.

01

A vetted bench

Every bartender on the bench passes a trial shift with the founder before they ever pour for a guest. The standard is set in person, not on paper.

02

Responsible service

We serve guests 21 and over, and we never serve a visibly intoxicated guest. A good evening ends well because the bar keeps it that way.

03

Your alcohol, our bar

The host provides the bottles, or we purchase at cost - we run everything else: the tools, the ice, the bar itself, setup to breakdown.
